Art of Kinetik – Mazokist

Owing much to the styling of gentlemen's crafts from the 1920's, Mazokist is a contemporary take on the classic luxury day cruiser. An elegant 30 feet long with a top speed of 42 knots, it is without equal in its class. Exquisitely crafted, it features such individual details as a bespoke steering wheel and a unique cockpit table. Outperforming all rivals, it delivers the smoothest and most comfortable of rides.

Mercedes Benz - Arrow 460

The luxury motor yacht “Arrow460–Granturismo” designed by Mercedes-Benz Style embarks on its maiden voyage off the coast of Nice. The first example of this new yacht model is named “Mercedes”, and true to the tradition of the name it founds a completely new boat concept. The 14-metre-long, 706 kW (960 hp) “Silver Arrow of the Seas” combines the performance of Mercedes-Benz sports cars with unique innovations from the boat industry. The yacht has classic automobile proportions that bring the hallmark Mercedes design idiom of sensual purity onto the water in a highly emotional and dynamic form.
